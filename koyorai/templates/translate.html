KoyorAI Translation/Transcription

Give the recorder access to your mircrophone and the widget will automatically identify the language being spoken and translate it to English in real time. If provided with English audio, it will instead transcribe the audio as it is being spoken.

Press record to have the translation service begin listening to your mircrophone and live-translating the audio in the window below. Press pause to temporarily stop recording/translating without resetting the current translation. Press download to download the audio of the current session along with an anotated subtitle English subtitle file. Press reset to clear the current recording and translation to start a fresh translation.
